01 / The thesis

Responsible AI isn’t the brake. Done right, it’s the steering. The thing that lets an organization move fast and mean it.

I’m an AI Governance leader and Technical Program Manager who designs and operationalizes responsible-AI frameworks across enterprise and public-sector environments. Today I serve as an AI Governance SME at Slalom Consulting and program-manage the firm’s technology partnership with Anthropic.

My work sits where policy meets production. I translate NIST AI RMF, ISO/IEC 42001, and EU AI Act requirements into plans engineering and product teams can actually execute. That includes governing ChatGPT Enterprise rollouts, agentic systems, and AI risk registers for clients in regulated industries. I bring clarity to ambiguity, and I keep delivery moving.
